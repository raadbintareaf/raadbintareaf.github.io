About

I am currently working at XU Exponential University of Applied Sciences in Potsdam, Germany. My research and teaching focus on large language models, explainable AI, and applied machine learning, with collaborations across academia, the public sector, and industry.

My current projects include:

  • XAI for Medical Health — collaborative research with Abu Dhabi University, Zayed University, and MIT.
  • FAIRWORK — examining platform work and digital labor conditions in partnership with WZB Berlin and the University of Oxford.
  • RITS — Resilient Infrastructure Technology Suite for decentralized water, food, and energy systems in Brandenburg.
  • QAVCM — Analytics system for quantitative, AI-assisted real-time evaluation of voluntary carbon market (VCM) certificates.

In my free time, I explore algorithmic trading and backtesting, blockchain and Web 3.0 technologies, fine-tuning cybersecurity and SIEM systems with LLMs, and green swarming IoT combined with AI.

Biography & Focus

I am a Professor of AI and Data Science stationed at XU Exponential University of Applied Sciences in Potsdam, Germany. My research and pedagogical frameworks operate at the intersection of deep learning interpretability, distributed systems architecture, and sustainable real-world applications.

My specialization encompasses Large Language Models (LLMs), Explainable AI (XAI), and applied machine learning. Beyond academia, I actively collaborate with the public sector and industry to deploy machine learning frameworks that address critical challenges. My independent research vectors include Web 3.0 technology integration, applied behavior analysis, and the fine-tuning of LLMs utilizing cybersecurity datasets for autonomous threat detection.

Teaching & Supervision

I instruct across Bachelor’s and Master’s degree programs, bridging theoretical computer science with practical toolchains. Responsibilities include curriculum design, thesis supervision, and executive training for over 200 global professionals.
